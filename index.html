Research

DOA Estimation Deep Unfolding

Adaptive DOA Estimation via Spherical Microphone Arrays

Developing a deep unfolding framework for direction-of-arrival estimation using spherical harmonic domain representations. The approach integrates algorithm-specific prior knowledge into a trainable network structure with per-layer parameters, targeting IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing.

Beamforming Spatial Audio

Region-of-Interest Beamforming

Design and analysis of adaptive beamformers for flexible spatial region selection, with applications to immersive audio capture and acoustic scene analysis. Includes real-time implementation on Speedgoat hardware and evaluation with Neumann KU100 binaural microphones.

Adaptive Filters LMS / NLMS / RLS

Adaptive Algorithm Design

Investigation of variable step-size adaptive filtering strategies including DVSS-NLMS and LMP-LMS frameworks for acoustic echo cancellation and active noise control in reverberant environments.
